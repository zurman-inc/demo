Hofstadter Sequence Demo f(n) = a(n) - Q(n)

$$ Q(n) = \begin{cases} 2 & \text{if } n=1 \\ 2 & \text{if } n=2 \\ 1 & \text{if } n=3 \\ Q(n-Q(n-1)) + Q(n-Q(n-2)) & \text{if } n \geq 4 \end{cases} $$
$$ a(n) = \begin{cases} 1 & \text{if } n=1 \\ 1 & \text{if } n=2 \\ 2 & \text{if } n=3 \\ a(a(n-1)) + a(n-a(n-1)) & \text{if } n \geq 4 \end{cases} $$

About this visualization

The heart shape is formed by plotting f(n) = a(n) − Q(n) for n = 1, 2, …, N, where a(n) is the Hofstadter–Conway sequence and Q(n) is Hofstadter's Q-sequence. Both are self-referential integer sequences.

Controls:

  • Points (N) — Number of sequence terms plotted (more = denser shape)
  • Rotation — Rotate the heart in degrees
  • Stretch — Vertical scale factor
  • FORMULAS — Toggle the recurrence formulas for Q and a
  • Color — Choose the plot color
